## Document Structure
Major Divisions of Subjects will have their own folders.

Subdivisions will have their own markdown documents, or sub-folders as appropriate.

EX:
- History:
    - Prehistory
    - Ancient History
        - Mesopotamia
        - China
- Mathematics:
    - Arithmetic
    - Calculus
        - Vector Calculus
